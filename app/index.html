Windy City Wig & Weave

Chicago's Premier Wig & Weave Works Since 1946

The Most Trusted Name In Quality Wigs

Our founder, Chadwick Wentz, learned wig-making from the old French masters during his spare time in Allied-liberated France towards the end of World War 2.

He put his hard-won knowledge to work immediately after returning to his hometown and family in Chicago. The result was some of the finest wig-work seen on this side of the Atlantic, let alone Lake Michigan.
